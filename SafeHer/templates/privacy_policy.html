Privacy Policy

Welcome to SafeHer! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data. By using our services, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information when you use SafeHer:

  • Personal Information: Such as name, email address, phone number, and payment information.
  • Location Data: For providing real-time tracking services and location-based alerts.
  • Device Information: Information about your device, including IP address, operating system, and browser type.
  • Usage Data: Details about how you interact with our services, such as app features used and session times.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Our Services: Including real-time tracking, location alerts, and emergency notifications.
  • To Improve Our Services: To enhance our user experience, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • For Security and Fraud Prevention: To protect against unauthorized access and misuse of our services.
  • For Communication: To send you important updates, security notices, and customer support messages.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We use a variety of security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption, firewalls, and secure socket layer (SSL) technology. While we strive to safeguard your data, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we have about you.
  • Rectify: Correct any inaccuracies in your personal information.
  • Delete: Request the deletion of your personal data under certain conditions.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw any consent you have given for the collection or processing of your data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

6. Third-Party Services

We may share your information with third-party service providers that help us operate our services, such as payment processors and analytics providers. These third parties are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information and may only use it to perform their services for us.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the date of the last revision. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for updates.
